About Me

I consider myself a lifelong learner, and I am currently studying Web Development. My educational background includes a BA in Psychology and an MSEd in Organizational Leadership. I have several years of experience working in Higher Education, and multiple customer service roles.

In learning about myself over the years, I have realized that there are three characteristics that make a line of work fulfilling to me: the opportunities to build new projects or ideas, be creative, and restore things that are seemingly broken.

In my personal time I enjoy watching/reading sci-fi and fantasy, playing video games, disc golf, and cycling.
